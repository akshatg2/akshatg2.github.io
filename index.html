Introduction to the Data

The data is taken from the world bank website the link for the same is: https://databank.worldbank.org/source/jobs#

The data shows the average life expectancy of females in the countries listed in it from the year 1990 to 2015. The insights which I am trying to find is the change in the numbers, because this shows how the particular country worked in its healthcare and how developed it is. Usually countries who perform better in this statistics is considered for better investments in healthcare industry by world bank and there are more initiatives taken to further improve the life quality and standards in that region.
